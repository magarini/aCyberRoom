task #3

During the quarantine, we would like to listen to the stories the body has to say about these times. What do our hands have to say in a period that contact seems to be limited? What ways does the body invent to amuse itself? What is the relation between body and time suspension? How does it affect our perception of time and everyday movement?We invite you to start a quarantine diary focused on the body experience of it and share with us excerpts from the texts.
